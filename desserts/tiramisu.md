# Tiramisú recipe

Inspired by [https://veganonboard.com/easy-vegan-tiramisu/](https://veganonboard.com/easy-vegan-tiramisu/).

## Ingredients
  - Silken tofu
  - Soy cream
  - Savoiardi (ladyfinger cookies)
  - Coffee

## Preparation
  1. Refridgerate the coconut milk over night for better separation of the creamy and liquid parts.
  2. Brew the coffee.

### For the creamy layer
  4. Briefly drain the silken tofu, and put it in a blender bowl.
  5. Scoop off the creamy, fatty part from the top of the canned coconut milk (about 150g per can). Add it (the creamy part) to the blender.
  6. Add the sugar and blend until completely smooth.

### Assembly
  7. Cover the bottom of your tiramisu dish(es) in biscuits soaked in coffee. (See recipe notes)
  8. Cover the biscuits with a layer of the creamy mixture.
  9. Add more layers of biscuits and cream, finishing with a layer of cream. How many layers you end up with depends on the size dish(es).
  10. Using a fine sieve, sprinkle on cocoa powder, and grate some dark chocolate on top.
  11. Refrigerate to let flavours blend and the tiramisu stiffen up. For best results, we suggest chilling for at least 8 hours.
