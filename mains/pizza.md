# Italian Pizza Recipe

**Serves:** 2 pizzas | **Prep time:** 30 minutes (excluding rises)

## Ingredients

### For the dough:
- 500g all-purpose flour
- 300ml warm water
- 10g salt
- 7g instant yeast
- 15ml olive oil

### For the topping:
- 400g crushed tomatoes
- 250g mozzarella cheese
- 30g fresh basil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 30ml olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the dough:**
   - Mix warm water and yeast, let sit for 5 minutes
   - Combine flour and salt in a large bowl
   - Add yeast mixture and olive oil
   - Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic

2. **First rise:**
   - Place dough in oiled bowl, cover with damp cloth
   - Let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size

3. **Prepare the sauce:**
   - Heat olive oil in a pan
   - Sauté minced garlic for 1 minute
   - Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper
   - Simmer for 15 minutes

4. **Shape and top:**
   - Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F)
   - Divide dough into 2 portions
   - Stretch each into a round pizza base
   - Spread tomato sauce evenly
   - Tear mozzarella and distribute over sauce
   - Drizzle with olive oil

5. **Bake:**
   - Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling

6. **Finish:**
   - Remove from oven
   - Top with fresh basil leaves
   - Serve hot and enjoy!
