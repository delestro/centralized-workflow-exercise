# Vegetarian feijoada recipe

## Ingredients

- **400 g** black beans (dried or 2 cans, drained and rinsed)
- **200 g** smoked tofu or plant-based sausage, sliced
- **1** medium carrot, diced
- **1** bell pepper, diced
- **1** medium onion, chopped
- **3 cloves** garlic, minced
- **2** bay leaves
- **1 tablespoon** butter or plant-based butter
- **Salt and pepper** to taste
- **1 liter** water or vegetable broth
- **Cooked white rice** for serving
- **Orange slices** for serving (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Prepare Beans:**
   - If using dried beans, soak them overnight, then drain.
   - Add beans to a pot, cover with water, and simmer for about 45-60 minutes until tender. If using canned beans, skip this step.

2. **Cook Base:**
   - Heat butter or plant-based butter in a large pot over medium heat.
   - Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened.

3. **Add Vegetables:**
   - Add carrot, bell pepper, and smoked tofu or plant-based sausage.
   - Cook for 5-7 minutes until slightly browned and fragrant.

4. **Combine:**
   - Add the cooked (or canned) beans to the pot along with water or vegetable broth.
   - Stir in bay leaves, salt, and pepper.

5. **Simmer:**
   - Let the feijoada cook uncovered for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened and flavorful.

6. **Serve:**
   - Remove bay leaves before serving.
   - Serve hot with white rice and orange slices on the side.
   - Enjoy!
