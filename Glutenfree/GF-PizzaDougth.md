# Glutenfree Pizza Dougth recipe 

- 100g mays flour 
- 100g almond flour 
- 50g glutenfree oats
- 1 tsp bakingsoda 
- 2dl water 
- 1dl oil 

# instructions 
- grind the oats 
- mix the flour, bakingsoda and oats together
- then add water 
- when it looks smooth add the oil 
- let it rest
